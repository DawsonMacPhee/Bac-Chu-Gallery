Welcome to the Bachinski/Chu

The Bachinski/Chu Print Study Collection (B/Chu) is a remarkable student-initiated, student-supported, student-run, and student-focused resource containing over 2,200 fine art prints spanning the technological and aesthetic developments of fine art printmaking from the sixteenth century to today. It is located in the School of Fine Art and Music, on the second floor of Zavitz Hall on the University of Guelph campus.

Student/Faculty Access

Students and Faculty of the University of Guelph have full access to copyrighted materials in this catalogue through their Single Sign On (username and password). Be advised that this is for research purposes only. Publishing reproductions of works in the Bachinski/Chu Print Study Collection may be an infringement on copyright law. For more information, write to bchupadmin@uoguelph.ca.

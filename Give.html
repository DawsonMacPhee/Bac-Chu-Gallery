Sup​port the Bachinski/Chu

Primarily funded with proceeds of the biannual fall and winter SOFAM Student Print Show and Sale as well as cash and ‘in-kind’ donations from members of our community, the Bachinski/Chu Print Study Collection has contributed to award-winning exhibitions, hosts quality experiential learning opportunities, creates exciting research collaborations, and holds stimulating community events. 

Cash and In-Kind Donations 

As a student-run and student-funded organization, the B/Chu would be grateful for your support. What we need most are cash donations to help us to acquire and steward works of art and purchase necessary equipment to meet our teaching, learning, and research mandates. If supporting training artists, art historians, and curators is a priority for you, please consider donating to the B/Chu today. To access our gifts policy, please click

here.

Your contribution to the Bachinski/Chu Print Study Collection goes where it is needed most:

  • Developing exhibitions that instruct students in curatorial and educational practices, and encouraging exploration of the collection by viewers
  • Improving collection facilities and services
  • Acquiring and preserving works
